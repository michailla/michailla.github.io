Pros of Algorithms
Technological Innovations: To the average person, increased use of big data and algorithms means that more advancements
in technology. This can make life for the individual easier and more convenient.
"Logical" or "Objective" Decisions: Individuals can leave decisions up to algorithms and machines, such as marriage or career path,
for the most optimal results based on the collective and connected knowledge of algorithms.
Links all disciplines: When all elements of life, whether biological or mechanical, are boiled down into algorithms, a common
language can be used in all disciplines. This makes discussion and study between scholars simpler.
